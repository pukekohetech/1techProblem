Functional vs Physical Specifications Quiz

Decide whether each specification is Functional (what the product does or must achieve) or Physical (what the product is like in size, form, weight, surface, or appearance).

Functional = Does

Performance, safety, usability, reliability, sustainability in use, cost targets, maintenance, transport, and operation.

Physical = Is

Size, weight, shape, texture, finish, compactness, stability of form, visual qualities, and measurable physical features.
